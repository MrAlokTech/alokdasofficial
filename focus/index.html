Privacy Policy

What we collect

Nothing. This app runs entirely in your browser. No personal data, usage data, or analytics are collected or transmitted to any server.

Local storage

Your time preferences (selected sessions, custom time sessions, their order, and your light/dark mode choice) and YouTube watch history are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

YouTube content

This app allows you to load and play YouTube videos via the official YouTube IFrame API. We do not host, download, store, or re-serve any YouTube content. All videos are played directly from YouTube's own servers. We make no claim of ownership over any YouTube video, and do not receive, track, or benefit from any view counts or analytics associated with videos played through this app. YouTube playback is subject to YouTube's own Terms of Service.

Ambient sound credits

The ambient sound tracks (Rain, Bird, Night) included in this app are sourced from Pixabay. All credits belong to their respective creators on Pixabay. We make no claim of ownership over these audio files.

Third-party services

Google Fonts is loaded to render typography. This is a standard CDN request; Google may log your IP as part of font delivery. No other third-party services are used.

Cookies

No cookies are set by this application.
